Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how JiT Techlabs Pvt. Ltd ("Company", "we", "us", or "our"), operating under the brand JiT Finco, collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal and business information when you access or use our website www.jitfin.co and the JiT Finco platform ("Platform").

This policy is published in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the rules made thereunder, including the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and is aligned with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDPA") to the extent applicable.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

a) Information you provide directly

  • Contact information: name, email address, mobile number, company name (via contact forms)
  • Business identity documents: PAN, Aadhaar (where applicable for KYC), GST registration certificates, business registration documents
  • Financial documents: bank statements, GST returns, financial statements, invoices, and purchase orders submitted as part of the loan facilitation process
  • Account credentials: login information if you create an account on the Platform

b) Information collected automatically

  • Device and browser information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, referring URLs
  • Cookies and similar technologies: session cookies, analytics cookies, and functional cookies (see Section 7)

c) Information from third parties

  • Credit information from credit bureaus (with your consent and through our lending partners)
  • Business verification data from public registries (MCA, GSTN)
  • Information provided by anchor corporates as part of supply chain finance programme onboarding

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate supply chain finance transactions between you and lending partners
  • To verify your identity and assess eligibility for financial products
  • To process and transmit your loan applications and supporting documents to lending partners
  • To communicate with you about your applications, account, and our services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations, including KYC and AML requirements
  • To improve, maintain, and secure the Platform
  • To analyse usage patterns and optimise the user experience
  • To send you relevant updates about our services (with your consent, where required)

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with the following parties:

  • Lending partners: banks and NBFCs to whom your loan application and supporting documents are submitted for credit assessment and disbursal
  • Anchor corporates: the corporate entities through whose supply chain programme you are applying, to the extent necessary for programme administration
  • Service providers: third-party vendors who assist with technology infrastructure, data analytics, communication services, and customer support, bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Regulatory and legal authorities: where required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • Credit bureaus: to support credit assessment processes conducted by our lending partners

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers located in India. We implement reasonable security practices and procedures consistent with industry standards, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel only
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including:

  • Active account or application data: for the duration of your use of the Platform plus 5 years after your last interaction
  • Financial and KYC documents: as required by applicable laws and RBI guidelines (typically 5–8 years after the completion of the relevant transaction)
  • Contact form submissions: 2 years from the date of submission, unless a business relationship is established

After the applicable retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Cookies

The Platform uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. The types of cookies we use include:

  • Essential cookies: required for the Platform to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies: to understand how visitors interact with the Platform (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Functional cookies: to remember your preferences and settings

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Platform.

8. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Right to withdraw consent: withdraw your consent for data processing at any time, which may affect our ability to provide services
  • Right to grievance redressal: raise a complaint regarding the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 11.

9. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites, including those of our lending partners. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Platform after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
